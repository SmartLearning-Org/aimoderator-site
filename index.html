About AI Moderator

Empowering online discussions with AI-driven moderation for an engaging and inclusive experience

What is AI Moderator?

The AI Moderator is an intelligent system designed to facilitate structured, inclusive, and productive online discussions by providing guidance, sharing relevant information, and keeping the conversation on track.

Key Features
  • Simple and intuitive interface
  • Use it anywhere – Computers, tablets, and mobile phones.
  • Flexible dialogue – Supports both synchronous and asynchronous participation.
  • Text or voice input – Communicate through typing or speaking, based on preference.
  • Powered by advanced AI – Uses OpenAI's latest models.

About Us

SmartLearning

SmartLearning is a Danish provider of accredited online education and a digital learning resource center, jointly owned by eight universities of applied sciences in Denmark.

As part of our commitment to improving online education, we lead the AI Moderator project in partnership with the Digital Education Futures Initiative (DEFI) at the University of Cambridge. This collaboration explores how AI-supported dialogue can enhance student engagement, strengthen critical thinking, and foster more inclusive engagement.
